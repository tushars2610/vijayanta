About Us

Welcome!

Vijai Electronics, Roorkee have been at the forefront of instrumentation industry in India, providing the state-of-the-art Instruments & related services, since 1966. Vijai Electronics offer instruments in the field of Electrical, Electronics Engineering, under the brand name of ‘VIJAYANTA’. This is an ISO 9001: 2008 certified company with hundreds of satisfied customer in all over the country including IITs, R&D Institutions and leading Industries.
