Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd., formerly Vijay Electronics, boasts a 50-year legacy as a leading provider of multi-technical services in India since its establishment in 1965. Specializing in integrated solutions, we offer expertise in electrical, electronics, automation, networking, and operation and maintenance services. Our comprehensive approach covers consulting, installation, execution, and maintenance across commercial and industrial sectors. With numerous successful projects in various industries and the government sector, we hold authorized distributorships of renowned multinational companies. Our adaptable resource structure and nationwide presence have earned us a reputation for delivering high-quality services efficiently. Venturing into turnkey contracting, we offer complete infrastructure, OEM, and interior works. As Original Equipment Manufacturers, we bring extensive experience in manufacturing testing, measurement, and training equipment, catering to reputed institutions and departments nationwide.

The Inspiring Journey Of Vijayanta Technologies

â€‹Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd. traces its roots back to the humble beginning of the Vijay Electronics, founded by Mr. Ranvijay Singh (Managing Director) in June 1965 in Roorkee with limited resources but boundless determination. He started the firm as a cottage industry, designing products tailored to meet the specific requirements of his customers. It was entirely a One-man show in which aspiring to excel in the quality of his goods, Ranvijay Singh personally handled the product design, production, installations, and demonstrations for his esteemed clients. He burnt mid night oil to ensure the high standards of quality and performance of all his products. Initially, Vijay Electronics focused on manufacturing the very basic and small instruments used for testing, measuring, and educational purposes in the varied fields of Electronics, Physics, and Electrical Engineering. With the passage of time, the company gradually expanded in its infrastructure and manufacturing aspects . He realized that to support the growing business he now required a reliable and efficient team. He went in to constitute it with three members who assisted him. Driven by his passion and entrepreneurial spirit, Mr. Ranvijay Singh's industriousness and dedication led to herculean growth in sales. The demand for Vijay Electronics' quality products soared to the heights of success. Prestigious educational institutions such as IIT, and NIT, renowned research centers like ISRO, and space science, and technology centers were added to the company's clientage. In the coming times, the company also began to cater to the requirements of the industries such as railways and electricity departments. It attained overloads of appreciation for its performance and service quality and continues to do so till. Mr. Vaibhav Singh (Director), Mr.Ranvijay Singh's son, took over the responsibility of the business. His focus on product promotion and marketing contributed to the company's growth. With the passage of time, Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd. expanded its operations with the support of dedicated and capable staff. The team toils hard for the best output. Mr. Vaibhav Singh's dedication and determination resulted in an annual turnover increase of 40%. A glorious era! 50 long years saw the collaborated efforts of Mr. Ranvijay Singh and Mr. Vaibhav Singh and the Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd. establishing itself and creating a benchmark for the industry. In 2016, the company underwent a transformation and was converted into a private limited company, officially adopting the name Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd. Today, Vijayanta Technologies Pvt. Ltd. continues to swagger holding its head high and safeguarding the legacy of its founders by delivering highly innovative and dependable products to cater to the evolving needs of its customers. With a rich history of excellence and a commitment to quality, the company looks forward to a bright future of continuous growth and success.

Our Services
We offer a comprehensive range of services to our esteemed clients, covering Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) and Operation and Maintenance (OEM) services in Electrical and Mechanical works. Our commitment to safety ensures the most effective protection and loss prevention methods.

Distinguished in the private operation and maintenance sector, we deliver cutting-edge security and loss prevention services. Our unique approach involves integrated planning to meet the specific needs of our clients in electrical, electronic, and mechanical OEM and AMC services.

As a trusted service provider in the domain, we are widely recognized for our Electrical Testing Services. Our skilled and experienced professionals conduct services for high tension, low tension, and electrical flow, addressing various needs such as:
  • Electrical power and harmonic testing for power quality
  • Circuit fault detection
  • DG efficiency and consumption calculations
  • Transformer testing, oil filtration, and quality assessment
  • Panel testing
  • Capacitor efficiency
